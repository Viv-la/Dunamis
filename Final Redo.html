Who is MsEpsi

Frida is a dynamic, confident and self-motivated Tech Entrepreneur and business coach. She has registerded several years of experience in using technology to enhance business creation, administration, operations and finance. She's is a founding member and Chief Operations Officer and Finance in Dunamis Safety Nets and the Dunamis Entrepreneurial Ecosystem which is an entrepreneurial hub as well as an accredited consultant with the Promotion Agency for Small and Medium sized Enterprises (APME) Cameroon. Eposi is equally currently leading a youth employment and leadership organization called Junior Chamber International (JCI)Limbe Atlantic as Chapter President. And when she is not found in the leadership and entrepreneurship space, she's encouraging young people on her social media page #EpsiMotivates on becoming better versions of themselves and the essence of personal development and growth. Eposi is passionate about working with startups and building the capacity of emerging leaders and entrepreneurs through self development. She believes that a little guidance can bring out the best in everyone, thus always looking forward to working with young people and growing businesses.
